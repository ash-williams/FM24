DoCM Future Me'24 | Hackathon
Welcome to the MMU Future Me week hackathon. Hosted by the Department of Computing and Mathematics.
Throughout the week, you will work within a group to produce a solution to the brief given below. There are prizes available for the best solutions, and those who most closely meet the brief.
There are also a number of workshops and other events going on throughout the week. All of the details are given below.
Best of luck to all those competing.
